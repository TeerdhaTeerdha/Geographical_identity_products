AboutUs

Why Choose us?

The specialty of Andhra Pradesh GI (Geographical Indication) tags lies in their representation of the region's rich cultural heritage, traditional craftsmanship, and agricultural practices. GI tags are awarded to products that have a specific origin and possess unique qualities attributable to their place of origin. For Andhra Pradesh, these tags highlight both traditional crafts and food products with deep local significance.
